💡 Money-Saving Tips

🎫 Book Ahead

Vatican & Colosseum sell out! Pre-booking avoids queues and scalper prices.

🍕 Eat Like Locals

Naples pizza is €5-8. Skip tourist traps near attractions. Ask locals!

🚄 Train Deals

Trenitalia "Super Economy" fares are 50% off. Book 2-4 weeks ahead.

🎫 GYG Tours Value

GetYourGuide tours include transport + guide — no car rental, parking, or fuel hassle!

💳 Use Wise Card

Best exchange rates, no foreign transaction fees. Top up in HKD, spend in EUR.

🌅 Free Sights

Trevi Fountain, Spanish Steps, Piazzas are free. Sunsets are free!

💶 Currency Info

€ Euro (EUR) ~HKD 8.5 per €1

💳 Payment Tips

  • Cards widely accepted (Visa, Mastercard)
  • Small shops may be cash-only
  • Carry €100-200 cash for markets & tips
  • ATMs everywhere (avoid airport exchange)
