01

First Day in Rome

June 3, Wednesday
Roma
🏠
Tonight's Stay · Rome (3 nights) Rome Airbnb · near Santa Croce in Gerusalemme Piazza di Santa Croce in Gerusalemme, 1, Roma, Lazio 00185, Italia
09:00

Trevi Fountain

Throw a coin, make a wish to return to Rome! Visit early to beat the crowds

10:00

Spanish Steps & Piazza di Spagna

Stroll the elegant shopping district. Stop by Antico Caffè Greco (Via dei Condotti 86) — 200+ year-old café, perfect for a rest

11:30

Piazza del Popolo & Pincian Hill

Grand piazza with twin churches, then climb Pincian Hill for panoramic views over Rome

13:00

Lunch near Piazza di Spagna

Traditional Roman trattoria — try cacio e pepe or amatriciana

15:00

Villa Borghese Gardens

Leisurely stroll through Rome's most beautiful park — rent a rowboat on the lake or just relax in the shade

17:00

Explore Trastevere

Charming cobblestone lanes, ivy-covered facades, and local artisan shops in Rome's most photogenic neighborhood

20:00

Welcome Dinner in Trastevere

Try La Gattabuia — 1600s papal prison turned restaurant! Must-order: carbonara, stewed oxtail, roast lamb, Roman artichoke

🍧 Grab a Grattachecca (Roman shaved ice) at Sora Mirella nearby!

⛲ Trevi Fountain 🌿 Villa Borghese 🍝 Roman Dinner

Roma

Seafood Dinner

Fresh Mediterranean seafood, Neapolitan style

Anacapri & Villa San Michele

Beautiful gardens with panoramic views

18:30

Ferry back to Naples

Return to mainland

20:00

Dinner in Naples

More pizza? Why not! Or try sfogliatella pastry

🏛️ Pompeii Day Trip

June 9, Tuesday
Napoli
08:30

Hotel Breakfast

Fuel up for a full day of ancient history

09:30

🚄 Circumvesuviana to Pompeii

Local train from Napoli Centrale to Pompeii Scavi station

🚄 ~40 min, ~€4 one-way | Departs every 30 min
10:30

⭐ Pompeii Archaeological Park

Walk the streets of the ancient Roman city frozen in time by Mount Vesuvius in 79 AD — remarkably preserved houses, temples, theatres, and frescoes

🏛️ Must-sees: Forum, House of the Faun, amphitheatre, Villa of the Mysteries. Allow 3-4 hours minimum!

🎫 Book tickets — ~€18/person, book ahead to skip queues
14:00

Lunch near Pompeii

Local trattoria outside the park — try fresh buffalo mozzarella from nearby farms

16:00

Spaccanapoli & Naples Old Town

Back in Naples — wander the narrow streets of the historic center, browse artisan shops, and soak in the atmosphere

20:00

Last Naples Pizza Night

Final night in Naples — one more round of the world's best pizza! Try a fritta (fried pizza) for something different

🏛️ Pompeii 🏘️ Spaccanapoli 🍕 Last Pizza Night

🚄 Train to Puglia

June 10, Wednesday
Napoli → Bari
🏠
Tonight's Stay · Bari (2 nights) Bari Airbnb · Picone-Poggiofranco Via Giulio Petroni, 14/E · Bari, Puglia 70100, Italia (~3 km SE of Bari Centrale)
08:00

Check-out Naples Airbnb

Pack up, walk/taxi to Napoli bus station (Metropark Centrale, ~10 min)

08:30

🚌 Bus to Bari (FlixBus / Itabus)

Budget bus across southern Italy to Puglia

🚌 ~3h, €11-20 | Arrive Bari ~11:30
12:00

Check-in Bari Airbnb & Lunch

Taxi from bus station to Via Giulio Petroni 14/E (~10 min, ~€10). Drop luggage, then taxi/bus 2 to Bari Vecchia for a quick lunch in the old town

15:00

⭐ Train to Polignano → Old Town

20min train from Bari → explore the dramatic clifftop old town — whitewashed buildings over the turquoise Adriatic. Visit the Domenico Modugno (Volare!) statue

🚄 Bari→Polignano: €3-4, every 30min, ~20min
17:00

⭐ Lama Monachile Beach

The iconic beach between two cliffs — swim in crystal clear water under the historic Roman bridge. One of Italy's most photographed spots!

20:00

Cliff-edge Seafood Dinner in Polignano

Stay in Polignano for sunset dinner! Try orecchiette, raw sea urchin, and local Primitivo wine. Donna Gina (Lama Monachile views) or Trattoria il Grottone (sea panorama). Train back to Bari after (~20min)

🚄 Train 🏘️ Polignano a Mare 🏖️ Lama Monachile
09

⛵ Boat Tour & Alberobello

June 11, Thursday
Bari (base)
08:30

Puglia Breakfast

Pasticciotto (Puglia's famous custard pastry) and espresso

10:00

⭐ Train to Polignano → Boat Tour (Sea Caves & Swimming)

Train from Bari (~20min), then explore Polignano's stunning sea caves from the water — swim in hidden coves and see Lama Monachile from the sea!

🎫 Book on GetYourGuide — ~1.5h boat tour, ~€25-35/person
12:00

Lunch in Polignano

Fresh seafood at a cliff-edge restaurant — try the famous raw prawns of Polignano!

14:00

⭐ Alberobello Guided Tour from Bari (UNESCO Trulli Houses)

Unique whitewashed cone-roof trulli houses — nowhere else in the world looks like this! UNESCO World Heritage Site

🎫 Book on GetYourGuide — Half-day tour from Bari | Or FSE bus ~1h, €5-10
17:30

Explore Rione Monti (Trulli District)

Wander the iconic trulli streets, browse handmade souvenirs, taste local olive oil and wine

20:00

Farewell Puglia Dinner in Bari

Bari Vecchia old town — orecchiette alle cime di rapa, burrata, focaccia barese, and taralli with a glass of Negroamaro. Puglia does food right!

⛵ Boat Tour 🏘️ Alberobello 🍝 Farewell Dinner
