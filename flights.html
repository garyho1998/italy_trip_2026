💡 Flight Booking Tips

📅 Book Early

Book 2-3 months in advance for best prices. June is peak season for Italy tourism.

🔄 Multi-City Booking

Round trip to/from Rome FCO. All internal travel by train (Naples→Puglia→Rome).

🧳 Baggage Policy

Check baggage allowance - some airlines include 23kg checked bag, others charge extra.

⏰ Connection Time

Allow at least 2 hours for connections. Middle East hubs (Dubai, Doha, Istanbul) are efficient.

🛩️ Internal Flight

No internal flights needed! Book trains on Trenitalia or Italo for Naples→Bari and Bari→Rome.

🪑 Seat Selection

Book seats together early, especially for groups of 6. Aisle + window pairs work well.
