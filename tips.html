🎫 Puglia Tour Tips (GetYourGuide)

📋

Book Early

Book GetYourGuide tours 2-4 weeks ahead for best availability. June is peak season — popular tours sell out fast!

🔄

Free Cancellation

Most GetYourGuide tours offer free cancellation up to 24 hours before. Book now, adjust plans later if needed!

🚐

Hotel Pickup

Both tours include hotel pickup in an AC van — no need to arrange transport yourself. Confirm pickup location when booking.

🌋

Polignano Dining

Try Donna Gina (Lama Monachile views) or Trattoria il Grottone (sea panorama) for cliff-edge seafood. Book ahead in summer!

🚌

Bari Base

2 nights in Bari — bus arrives directly, Alberobello tours depart from here, and Bari is your FlixBus departure point back to Rome. Polignano is just 20min by train for beach + boat tour!

🚄 Train Travel

🎫

Book Trains Early

Frecciarossa high-speed trains are the best way Rome → Naples. Book 2-4 weeks ahead for cheapest "Super Economy" fares!

📱

Digital Tickets

Download e-tickets to your phone. QR codes work for validation. Have screenshot backup in case of no signal at station.

🍕 Food & Dining

☕

Coffee Culture

Stand at the bar (al banco) for 50% cheaper coffee. "Un caffè" = espresso. Cappuccino only before 11am! Never with pasta.

🍕

Naples = Pizza Paradise

You're in the birthplace of pizza! Try L'Antica Pizzeria da Michele or Sorbillo. Margherita DOP costs only €5-8!

🍝

Puglia Specialties

Try arancini (fried rice balls), pasta alla Norma, cannoli, and granita con brioche for breakfast!

💰

Coperto & Servizio

Coperto (€1-3 cover charge) is normal. "Servizio" is service charge - if included, no tip needed. Tipping isn't expected but appreciated.

⛪ Churches & Vatican

👔

Dress Code

Cover shoulders and knees - no exceptions! Carry a scarf/shawl. You will be turned away otherwise, even at the Vatican.

📸

Photography

No flash in churches. No photos in Sistine Chapel. Silence phones. Some churches charge €1-3 for photos.

🌊 Blue Grotto (藍洞) Tips

🌊

Weather Dependent

The Blue Grotto closes in rough seas or high tides. Go early morning (9-10am) for best chance. If closed, Capri has other beautiful caves!

🚤

What to Expect

Small rowboat enters through tiny opening (you lie flat!). Inside: magical blue light. Quick visit (~5 min inside) but unforgettable. Take motion sickness pills if sensitive.
