💡 Transport Tips

🚄 Train Booking

Book trains 2-4 weeks ahead for "Super Economy" fares. Tickets on Trenitalia/Italo apps.

✈️ Budget Airlines

Ryanair/easyJet have strict baggage rules. Book carry-on only or pay for checked bag.

🎫 GetYourGuide Tours

Book Puglia tours on GetYourGuide 2-4 weeks ahead. Free cancellation 24h before. Hotel pickup included!

⛴️ Blue Grotto

Weather dependent! Go early morning for calmer seas. Closed if waves are too high.

🏘️ Alberobello

Day trip from Polignano — GetYourGuide tour includes transport (~30-40min each way). Or take regional train via Bari.

🚕 Puglia Transport

Polignano is walkable! Regional trains connect to Bari (~20min). Taxis available for Alberobello if not doing a tour.
